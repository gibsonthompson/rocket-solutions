## 🎯 Opt-In Method Description (FOR TELNYX FORM)

**Description for Telnyx**:
```
Website-based opt-in with express written consent. Users complete a contact form 
on our website (rocketsolutions.com) that includes:

1. Required checkbox that must be actively checked by the user
2. Clear SMS consent language explaining message purpose, frequency, and rates
3. STOP and HELP keyword instructions
4. Links to Privacy Policy and Terms of Service
5. Statement that consent is not required for purchase

Upon form submission, we record:
- Timestamp of consent
- User's IP address
- User agent/device information
- Full consent text accepted

This creates a complete audit trail proving express written consent was obtained 
before sending any SMS messages.
```
